Carbonara

Description

Carbonara is a classic pasta dish that is simple and delicious.

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp. kosher salt
  • 3 oz. bacon
  • 2 oz. parmesan
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 lb. spaghetti

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil
  2. Cut bacon into strips
  3. Whisk 4 egg yolks and 2 whole eggs in a bowl. Add in cheese.
